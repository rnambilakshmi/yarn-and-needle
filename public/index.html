A Passion Woven from a Single Thread.

Hello, I'm the hands and heart behind Yarn and Needle. What began as a simple, meditative hobby has woven itself into the fabric of my life. There's a unique magic in transforming a single thread of yarn into something beautiful and tangible.

Each creation you see here is a piece of that journey—a story of quiet moments, patient hands, and the joy of making. My hope is that these pieces bring as much warmth and character to your life as they have brought to mine. Thank you for being a part of my story.

The Art of the Craft

Every piece is a labor of love, following a thoughtful, meticulous process.

1. Design & Vision

It all starts with an idea, sketching out a design and carefully selecting the perfect, high-quality yarn for the project.

2. Meticulous Crafting

With hook in hand, I patiently bring the design to life, stitch by stitch. This is where the magic happens.

3. Final Touches

Each piece is finished with care, ensuring every detail is perfect before it is beautifully packaged and sent to you.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I care for my crochet item?

For most items, I recommend gentle hand washing with a mild detergent and laying flat to dry. This will ensure your handmade treasure lasts for years to come.

Do you ship across India?

Yes, absolutely! I ship to all locations within India. Shipping details and costs will be confirmed when you place your order on WhatsApp.

Can I request a custom order?

Of course! I love bringing custom ideas to life. Please reach out via the contact section below, and we can discuss your vision.
